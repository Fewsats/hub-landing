PayWithHub Extension Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Use

  1. API Key: We collect and store your API key to authenticate requests to our service. This key is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted except when making authorized API calls.
  2. Browsing Activity: Our extension processes L402 links on web pages you visit. This processing occurs locally on your device and does not involve sending your browsing history to our servers.
  3. Transaction Data: When you make purchases through our extension, transaction details are sent to our servers to process the payment and provide access to content.
  4. Location Data: We do not collect or store any location data.
